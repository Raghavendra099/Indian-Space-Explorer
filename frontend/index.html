SPACE ODYSSEY

ISRO Timeline

Six decades of reaching for the stars.

🛰
1975

Aryabhata Satellite

India's first satellite, named after the ancient mathematician, launched by a Soviet rocket.

🚀
1980

SLV-3 — Rohini Launch

India joins the elite club of nations capable of launching satellites with its own rocket.

🌙
2008

Chandrayaan-1

Discovered water molecules on the Moon's surface — a watershed moment in lunar science.

🔴
2013

Mars Orbiter Mission

First Asian nation to reach Martian orbit. Mission cost less than the Hollywood film "Gravity."

🌕
2023

Chandrayaan-3 Landing

Vikram lander achieves first-ever soft landing near the lunar south pole. Pragyan rover deploys.

☀️
2023

Aditya-L1

India's first dedicated solar mission, studying the Sun from the L1 Lagrange point.

👨‍🚀
2025+

Gaganyaan

India's first crewed space mission will send Vyomanauts to low Earth orbit.
